NeonFrame is a fresh Cyberpunk-inspired UI for Roblox that really lights up your game. Built from the ground up in Lua, it delivers those slick, neon-banded panels, interactive buttons, toggles, sliders, and dropdowns—complete with smooth, lively animations. Every bit of it feels like it pulses with energy, from tabs that glow to notifications that slide in and bounce around like holographic alerts straight out of a neon-lit city.

With NeonFrame, you can create dashboards, menus, and control panels that fit perfectly in any high-tech dystopian world. It’s all set up for you—tabs to keep things organized, sections to group features, and interactive controls that immediately respond so that every click or adjustment just feels right.

It’s built for style and usability at the same time. Imagine deep, dark backgrounds set off by flashes of electric blue, hot magenta, and glowing cyan. The whole thing screams futuristic cyberpunk but runs smooth, never dragging down performance. Even the notifications do more than just pop up—they slide in with personality, grabbing your attention without losing their cool.

NeonFrame goes beyond just being a set of menus or buttons. It’s there to transform your hubs, admin panels, or roleplay games into something that feels alive and electrifying. You end up with interfaces that look incredible and immerse players in a glowing, digital metropolis where design and technology are totally in sync.
